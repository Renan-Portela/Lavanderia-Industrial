# Quickstart: Managerial Reports Refactor

## Verification Steps

### Test 1: Aesthetic Parity
1. Visit the Dashboard.
2. Navigate to Reports.
3. Verify that the header structure (Title + Mini-stat cards) is identical.
4. Verify that status colors (e.g., green for Expedido) match on both pages.

### Test 2: KPI Dynamics
1. Check the Reports header cards.
2. Verify the **Volume Processado** matches the sum of quantities in the database.
3. Verify the **Lead Time Médio** reflects the processing speed accurately.

### Test 3: Side-by-Side Drill-down
1. Click an Order ID in the Reports table.
2. Verify the side-card appears on the right with the full order details.
3. Click "Copiar" in the card and verify the clipboard.

### Test 4: OKR Progress
1. Check the OKR indicator (Comparison vs. Last Month).
2. Verify the percentage shows a realistic trend based on recent data.

### Test 5: Time Filtering
1. Apply a "Monthly" filter.
2. Verify the table and the header KPIs update to reflect only that month's data.
